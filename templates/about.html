This is an Unofficial Habits app based off the best selling book "Atomic Habits"

If you don't have it I recommend clicking the image below,to first get the book as I don't explain any of the concepts used in this app.

Already have or read the book ? Keep reading...

You'll want to go straight to "Create a Habit" or "Break a Habit",fill out the questionaire, Then you're new habit will appear in the "Habit List".

You'll then have a habit sheet, with the option to print it out, make changes or review the Habit on the App.
